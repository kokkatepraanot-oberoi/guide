Oberoi International School · JVLR Campus

Teacher Appraisal 2025–26
Step-by-Step Guide

📅 Due: April 30, 2026
🔗 Open appraisal website
All teachers need to complete the Final Self-Assessment and Final Evaluation on the website.
1

Go to ois-appraisal.streamlit.app

  1. Click 🖐 Login with Google school account
  2. Select your @oberoi-is.org school account
  3. You will land on your dashboard automatically
What you will see
The login page — click the Google button
2

Complete your Final Self-Assessment

Due April 30, 2026
  1. In the sidebar menu, select Self-Assessment (Initial & Final). Your Initial ratings from Sep 2025 are shown below.
  2. Scroll to Final Self-Assessment — Apr 2026. Click each domain (A–F) to expand it.
  3. For each strand, select your rating using the horizontal radio buttons: HE · E · IN · DNMS.
  4. New to OIS or did not complete the Initial Self-Assessment in Sep 2025? Simply complete your Final Self-Assessment independently.
  5. Use the 💾 Save Draft button in the sidebar to save progress and return later — your work is not lost.
  6. Once all 53 strands are rated, the Submit button turns active. Click ✅ Submit.
⚠️ Once you confirm submission it cannot be undone — review all ratings before clicking Yes
What you will see
Sidebar: progress bar + Save Draft button
Submit activates once all 53 strands are rated
3

Complete your Final Evaluation section

  1. Select Final Evaluation from the menu. This tab only appears after your Final Self-Assessment is submitted.
  2. Enter your Subject Area/Grade Level (type it in — e.g. English, Mathematics, Science).
  3. Student Survey Feedback — summarise what students said about your teaching and what steps you took.
  4. Overall Reflection on the school year — what you learned, improved, and want to develop.
  5. Click 💾 Save to save a draft. When ready, click ✅ Submit to appraiser. A confirmation prompt appears — click Yes, submit now to confirm.
What you will see
Confirmation prompt — review carefully before clicking Yes
4

Wait for your appraiser to complete their section

  1. After you submit, your appraiser is notified and completes their domain ratings and overall comments.
  2. Your appraiser will schedule an in-person meeting with you to discuss the evaluation.
What you will see
This message appears while your appraiser works on their section
5

View your evaluation & download your summary

  1. After your in-person meeting, your appraiser will click Share Evaluation with you in their system.
  2. You will then see your full evaluation under Final Evaluation → Appraiser Review — domain ratings, overall rating, and comments.
  3. Click 📄 Download My Evaluation Summary (DOCX) to save a copy for your own records.
  4. Appraisal cycle is now complete.
🎉 Once the evaluation is shared with you, the process is finished
